It's been years since I got this domain of my own.

Back in 2018, I was already sure that I really want to improve my website and use it as a place to store the quotes, links, thoughts, and blog posts that I found interesting. I wanted it to become a small place of the internet that felt like mine.

I was always afraid to post for a few reasons:

1. I did not think I was good at writing.
2. I felt like my thoughts were too simple.
3. I was worried that people might judge what I wrote.
4. I kept waiting until my website looked “ready.”
5. I kept telling myself I would start once I had something more important to say.

Looking back, I think I was overthinking it.

The truth is, writing is something I can only improve by actually doing it. I do not need every post to be perfect. I do not need every thought to be profound. I just need to start.

So this year, 2026, I finally decided to write.

This blog will probably be a mix of things: notes about software engineering, AI, books I’m reading, ideas I want to remember, personal reflections, links I find useful, and maybe even small thoughts that do not fit anywhere else.

I want this site to become a public log. A place where I can document what I’m learning, what I’m building, what I’m thinking about, and how I’m changing over time.

At last, I’m writing.

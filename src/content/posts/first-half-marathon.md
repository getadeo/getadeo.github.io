Crossed the finish line at 2:05. Not a record-breaking time, but finishing felt incredible.

## What worked

- Slow long runs on weekends built my endurance without injury
- Fueling with gels every 45 minutes kept energy steady
- Running with a pace group kept me honest in the first half

## What I'd change

- Start training earlier — 12 weeks felt tight
- More hill work — the last 5K was brutal on the inclines

Already eyeing a full marathon next year.

I got into chess around two years ago, and it consumed me. I enjoy playing, but it takes too much time for me. This app will keep me occupied and scratch that chess itch while I'm on a plane, resting, or doing number two 😂.

The goal is simple: "Place every piece so none can capture another." Sounds simple, right? Wait until you get to the harder puzzles.
